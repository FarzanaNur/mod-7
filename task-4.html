Mixed Chow-mein

Ingredients:

Chow-mein Noodles,Boneless Chicken,Egg(optional),Choice of Vegetable,Mushroom(optional),Black Paper,Salt,Soya Sauce,Butter/ Choice of Oil,Oregano(optional),Chilli-flex.

Process:

First, boil water, and then add the noodles along with a tip of oil and a pinch of salt; the salt will help season the noodles and prevent them from sticking together. Next, add butter to a pan and, over medium heat, scramble the egg with a bit of salt and pepper. Once the egg is done, add the chicken, mushrooms, and vegetables, seasoning with salt and pepper according to taste. After a few minutes, stir in the sauce mixture (dark soy sauce, light soy sauce, oyster sauce, Worcestershire sauce [optional], tomato sauce, and sugar to taste), and allow it to simmer for a few minutes. If the mixture becomes too thick, you can add a little of the noodle cooking water. Meanwhile, check if the noodles are 90% cooked; if they are, drain them and let them cool. Remember, never overcook the noodles! Finally, add the noodles to the chicken and vegetable mixture, toss everything together, and adjust the seasoning with salt, pepper, and chili flakes. Your dish is now ready to enjoy!

Chicken Mo-Mo

Ingredients:

For Filling:

Boneless chicken, Ginger-Garlic paste/powder,Soya sauce,Choice of Vegetables (for this recipe i used carrots only),salt,paper.

For dough:

All purpose flour,salt,oil.

For Sauce:

Chili flex, Tamarind,Water,Oil.

Process:

For the mo-mos, start by preparing the filling using finely chopped boneless chicken, ginger-garlic paste or powder, soy sauce, finely grated carrots (or any other vegetables of your choice), salt, and pepper. Mix everything well and set aside. For the dough, combine all-purpose flour with a pinch of salt, a little oil, and enough water to form a smooth dough, then let it rest for about 30 minutes. Roll out the dough into small circles, place a spoonful of the chicken and vegetable filling in the center, and fold the edges together to seal each mo-mo. Steam the mo-mos for 10-15 minutes until the dough is cooked through. For the sauce, mix chili flakes, tamarind, water, and oil, then heat it until well combined. Serve the mo-mos hot with the prepared sauce.
